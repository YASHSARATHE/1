  • Day 03: Morning After breakfast Departure for Menhdipur Balaji. After darshan proceed for Khatu ji. Evening reach Khatu ji, check in hotel overnight stay.

  • Day 04: Morning After Khatuji darshan, Proceed for Pushkar.Reach Pushkar, check in hotel keep luggage ,visit Bramha ji Temple and holy Sarovar. Night stay Pushkar.

  • Day 06: Morning after Breakfast visit Ummed Bhawan palace then proceed for Jaisalmer. on the way visit Ramdewara. Night stay Jaisalmer.

  • Day 07: After break fast Half day Jaisalmer local sightseeing. After lunch visit Sam Dunes. Night stay in tent.

  • Day 08: After breakfast departure for Abu Road . Reach Abu Road , checkin hotel overnight stay.

  • Day 09: After Breakfast Mount Abu local sightseeing. After lunch Departure for Udaipur. Reach Udaipur, checkin hotel overnight stay.

  • Day 10: After Breakfast Whole day Udaipur local sight seen. Night stay Udaipur.

  • Day 11: After Breakfast departure for Chittorgarh, on the way visit Nathdwara and sawariya seth ji.Reach Chittorgarh .There visit Chittorgarh fort. Evening Deaparture for Bhopal by Ajmer-Hyderabad exp(12719).
